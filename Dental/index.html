Everything You

We believe informed patients make the best decisions. Here are the questions we hear most often.

Ask Us Anything

Most patients benefit from a routine checkup and cleaning every six months. However, if you have a history of gum disease, cavities, or other ongoing issues, Dr. Mehra may recommend visits every three to four months. We'll create a schedule tailored specifically to your oral health needs.

Dental implants are suitable for most adults with good general health and sufficient jawbone density. Ideal candidates are non-smokers without uncontrolled diabetes. We conduct a thorough consultation including 3D scans to assess your suitability. If bone loss has occurred, bone grafting can often make implants possible.

Traditional braces use metal brackets and wires permanently bonded to the teeth — they're highly effective for complex cases. Clear aligners (like Invisalign) are removable, virtually invisible trays changed every 1–2 weeks. Aligners offer aesthetic advantages and easier cleaning, but braces may be more appropriate for severe misalignment. We'll recommend the best option after a full assessment.

Yes — we are in-network with most major insurance providers including Delta Dental, Cigna, Aetna, MetLife, and BlueCross BlueShield. Our front-desk team will verify your benefits before your appointment and provide a clear breakdown of any out-of-pocket costs. We also offer flexible payment plans through CareCredit.

Professional in-office whitening (which we use) is very safe and supervised by Dr. Mehra to protect your gum tissue and enamel. Results typically last 1–3 years depending on diet and oral hygiene. We also provide custom take-home trays for touch-ups. Over-the-counter products are less effective and carry higher risks if misused.

Your first appointment is a comprehensive new-patient exam (about 60–90 minutes). We'll take digital X-rays, perform a full oral health assessment including a gum evaluation and oral cancer screening, and have an in-depth conversation about your dental history and goals. Dr. Mehra will then present a personalized treatment plan with no pressure to proceed immediately.
