Rich Dad Poor Dad

Robert

Robert Kiyosaki is the best-selling author of Rich Dad Poor Dad, a book that fundamentally changed how millions of people view personal finance. Born in Hawaii, Kiyosaki draws on his upbringing to contrast two different philosophies: the "Poor Dad" (his biological father, an educator who valued job security) and the "Rich Dad" (his friend’s father, an entrepreneur who valued financial independence).
